What Is eero Router Setup?
==========================

The **eero router setup** refers to the installation and configuration process required to activate your eero WiFi system. Unlike traditional routers that rely on browser-based configuration pages, eero uses a mobile application to guide users through setup.

The setup process connects your modem to the eero device, establishes your WiFi network, and allows you to customize network settings.

Requirements Before Starting eero Router Setup
==============================================

Before beginning the eero router setup, make sure you have:

- An active internet modem
- Your eero router device
- Power adapter and Ethernet cable
- A smartphone or tablet
- The eero mobile application installed
- A stable internet connection

Preparing these items ensures the eero router setup runs smoothly without interruptions.

How to Perform eero Router Setup
=================================

Step One: Connect eero to Modem
-------------------------------

Unplug your modem from power. Connect the eero router to the modem using the Ethernet cable. Plug the modem back in and wait until it fully powers on. Then plug in your eero device.

Step Two: Download and Open the App
-----------------------------------

Download the official eero app on your smartphone or tablet. Open the application to begin the eero router setup process.

Step Three: Create or Sign In to Account
----------------------------------------

You will need to create an account or sign in using your registered email address or phone number. Verification is completed using a code sent to your device.

Step Four: Follow On-Screen Instructions
----------------------------------------

The app will guide you through each step of the eero router setup, including detecting your device and confirming connection to the internet.

Step Five: Create WiFi Network
------------------------------

Choose a network name and password during the eero router setup. Make sure to create a strong password for better security.

Once completed, your WiFi network will be active and ready for device connections.

Troubleshooting eero Router Setup Issues
========================================

No Internet Connection
----------------------

If the app cannot detect internet access, check modem cables and restart both modem and eero device.

Device Not Found
----------------

Ensure Bluetooth is enabled on your smartphone and that you are close to the eero router.

Slow Connection After Setup
---------------------------

Try repositioning the eero device in a central location away from thick walls or electronic interference.

App Not Working
---------------

Update the eero app to the latest version and restart your smartphone if needed.
